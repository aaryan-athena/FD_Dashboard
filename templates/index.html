About SteadyMatch

More than just technology - it's peace of mind

Our Story

SteadyMatch is a smart fall-detection solution designed to bring safety, speed, and peace of mind to caregiving.

Connected to a Raspberry Pi and powered by a "You Only Look Once" (YOLO) model, the app instantly detects when a person falls. Within seconds, it captures and saves a 10-second video clip—five seconds before and after the incident—and alerts the primary caregiver. This ensures help arrives fast, with the context of the situation clearly visible.

The Inspiration

The inspiration behind SteadyMatch came from a personal story—a grandfather's fall that went unnoticed. That moment highlighted a widespread problem among senior citizens: the fear of falling and the stress it brings to both residents and caregivers.

By combining engineering with empathy, SteadyMatch was built to make caregiving proactive rather than reactive, empowering seniors to live freely and caregivers to respond with confidence.

Our Mission

To create a world where seniors can live independently with dignity, knowing that help is always just seconds away when they need it most.
